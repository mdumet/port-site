Alfred Angelo - Mobile Design


Overview

This mobile design was done for Alfred Angelo in 2014.
The objective was to create a mobile platform where they could showcase beautiful photographs of their bridal collections and provide details about the dresses, but most importantly they wanted to make it easier for brides to share these photographs on social media and increase mobile traction to their business.
